Instructions

You will see 100 pairs of faces. Please finish within 30 minutes.

Try to make sense of what most people think and we will compare your responses with others.

You may see the same pair more than once (the left-right order might be flipped). Watch carefully and make consistent choices.

Your HIT will be accepted if more than 70% of your choices align with most people and more than 70% of your own choices are consistent when seeing the same pair (choosing the same face in spite of the potential flipped left-right order).

Press A to choose the left face, press B to choose the right face.

Don't press until both faces are loaded.

Have fun :)
